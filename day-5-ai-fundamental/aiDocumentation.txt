AI Concept Documentation---

1. What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a technology that allows machines or computers to think, learn, and make decisions similar to humans.

AI systems can:

learn from data,
recognize patterns,
solve problems,
understand language,
make predictions.

Simple Real-Life Examples of AI----
ChatGPT
Google Assistant
Siri
Netflix recommendations
YouTube recommendations
Face Unlock
Self-driving cars
AI image generators

2. Difference Between AI, ML, and Deep Learning
Artificial Intelligence (AI)-----

The broad field of making machines intelligent.
Example:
Chess-playing computer

Machine Learning (ML)------
A subset of AI where machines learn from data automatically.
Example:
Spam email detection

Deep Learning (DL)------

A subset of ML using neural networks with many layers.

Example:
AI image generation
voice assistants


Simple Flow----
Artificial Intelligence
        ↓
Machine Learning
        ↓
Deep Learning



3. How AI Works-------

AI works in 4 major steps:

Step 1 — Data Collection

AI needs data to learn.

Examples:

images
text
videos
user behavior
transactions
Step 2 — Training

AI studies patterns in data.

Example:

thousands of cat images → learns what a cat looks like
Step 3 — Model Creation

After training, AI creates a mathematical model.

The model becomes capable of:

prediction,
classification,
generation.
Step 4 — Prediction / Output

AI uses learned knowledge to answer or predict.

Example:

recognizing a face
answering questions

4. Machine Learning (ML)

Machine Learning teaches computers to learn automatically from data without explicit programming.

Types of Machine Learning
A. Supervised Learning

AI learns using labeled data.

Example:

Input → Output already known

Examples:

spam detection
price prediction
disease detection

B. Unsupervised Learning

AI learns patterns from unlabeled data.

Examples:

customer grouping
recommendation systems
C. Reinforcement Learning

AI learns using rewards and punishments.

Examples:

game AI
robotics
self-driving cars
5. Deep Learning

Deep Learning uses Neural Networks inspired by the human brain.

Used for:
image recognition
voice recognition
AI chatbots
autonomous vehicles

14. What is AI Training?-------

Training means teaching AI using large amounts of data.

Example:

train AI on thousands of dog images.

After training:

AI can recognize dogs in new images.

15. What is Overfitting?---------

Overfitting happens when AI memorizes training data too much and performs poorly on new data.

16. What is AI Bias?-------

AI bias happens when training data is unfair or unbalanced.

Example:

biased hiring AI system
